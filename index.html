SEC Hopes to Make Succession Plans Mandatory for Financial Advisors

The SEC wants to make succession plans a requirement, and it could happen sooner than you think. This past December, SEC Chairman Mary Jo White said, “[We are] developing a recommendation to require investment advisers to create transition plans to prepare for a major disruption in their business” (sec.gov). This transition (succession) rule is on the SEC’s regulatory calendar for proposal in October. Read the original article for more details here: Investment News
